Responsive

And ready for what comes next. Formstone components are designed mobile-first to ensure fast, usable interfaces, no matter the screen size.

Modular

Maintaining a project means adding functionality as it matures. Components are name-spaced for minimal overlap with your existing styles and scripts.

Lightweight

Component files are minified and dependencies kept to a minimum so sites load quickly and reliably for every user.

Open Source

Missing a feature? Notice a bug in IE 10? Components are MIT Licensed and individually maintained so you can give a little back or make them your own.

Automated

Formstone components are compiled with Grunt and deployable with Bower to ensure code quality and maintain simple implementation.

Browser Support

The web is larger then WebKit and Formstone helps achieve cross browser support for your site, while maintaining the interactions users expect.
